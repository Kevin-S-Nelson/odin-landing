Once you click this blue pill, you will never be able to go back to your "magic keyboard" again!
The Anatomy of a Mechanical Keyboard
First you must decide which layout you would like. Think of this like HTML, it lays out the basic structure with minor tweaks from board to board.
Next you must choose your switches. These are kind of like the JavaScript of your keyboard, they have a huge impact on the feel, but require of lot of time consuming work to get it just right.
Finally you get to choose your keycaps! Like CSS, it is the fun part because there are so many customization options available to make it uniquely yours.
If you somehow still have money, you can get a coiled cable. This is like backend development, only the truly committed make it this far!
